About Us
What sets us apart?
Courteous & Knowledgeable Staff?
Hooked on Fish & Corals in Miami is your South Florida Aquarium store. Our staff has over 10 years of experience providing information on aquatic animal life, along with maintenance and preservation. Throughout the years our staff has built an honest rapport with our clients, offering them helpful and caring advice.
Wide Variety of Marine Life?
Our store has over 2000 gallons of saltwater aquariums full of healthy marine life including fish (from the traditional to exotic), invertebrates, and live corals. If we do not have it in stock we will find a way to get it for you. Please come and check out the wide variety we have to offer.
How about supplies?
Hooked on Fish & Corals has the widest variety of aquarium products and supplies for your aquatic care needs. Our staff will be able to let you know exactly will help you choose the right products for your specific aquarium.
Want a little something special?
Hooked on Fish & Corals actually specializes in custom aquariums including complete set up (optional monthly maintenance can be added on). Come in with an idea and we will do what everything in our power to make your vision a reality.
